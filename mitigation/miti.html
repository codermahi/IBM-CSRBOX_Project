Mitigation of Climate Change

Renewable Energy

Renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydro power can significantly reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Energy Efficiency

Improving energy efficiency in buildings, industries, and transportation can lead to substantial energy savings and emission reductions.

Afforestation

Planting trees and restoring forests can absorb CO2 from the atmosphere, helping to mitigate climate change.

Policy Actions

Effective policy measures are essential to drive systemic changes and achieve long-term climate goals.

  • Carbon pricing
  • Subsidies for clean energy
  • Regulations and standards
